# Online Learning Platform Requirements

## Project Overview
Develop an online learning platform that enables instructors to create and publish
courses, and allows students to enroll, learn, and track their progress. The
platform should support video content, quizzes, assignments, and certificates.

## User Types
1. Students - Users who enroll in and take courses
2. Instructors - Users who create and manage courses
3. Administrators - Platform operators who manage users and content

## Student Features

### Course Discovery
- Students can browse courses by category, topic, and difficulty level
- Students can search for courses using keywords
- Students can view course previews including syllabus and instructor info
- Students can see course ratings and reviews from other students
- Students can filter courses by price (free, paid), duration, and language

### Learning Experience
- Students can watch video lectures with playback controls
- Students can take notes while watching lectures
- Students can download supplementary materials (PDFs, slides)
- Students can complete quizzes and receive immediate feedback
- Students can submit assignments for instructor review
- Students can track their progress through course completion percentage
- Students can resume courses from where they left off
- Students can participate in course discussion forums

### Certificates and Progress
- Students receive certificates upon course completion
- Students can view their learning history and achievements
- Students can share certificates on social media and LinkedIn
- Students can set learning goals and receive reminders

## Instructor Features

### Course Creation
- Instructors can create courses with multiple sections and lectures
- Instructors can upload video content with automatic transcription
- Instructors can add supplementary materials to lectures
- Instructors can create quizzes with multiple question types (MCQ, true/false, short answer)
- Instructors can set prerequisites for courses
- Instructors can price their courses or offer them for free

### Student Management
- Instructors can view enrolled students and their progress
- Instructors can send announcements to all enrolled students
- Instructors can respond to student questions in discussions
- Instructors can grade assignments and provide feedback
- Instructors can view course analytics (enrollments, completion rates, ratings)

## Administrator Features
- Administrators can manage user accounts (create, suspend, delete)
- Administrators can review and approve new courses
- Administrators can handle content moderation and reports
- Administrators can view platform-wide analytics
- Administrators can configure payment settings and commission rates
- Administrators can manage course categories and tags

## Technical Requirements

### Performance
- Video streaming should start within 3 seconds
- Page load time should be under 2 seconds
- System should support 10,000 concurrent video streams

### Security
- All user data must be encrypted
- Payment processing must be PCI-DSS compliant
- Content must be protected against unauthorized downloads

### Scalability
- Platform should handle 100,000 registered users
- Video storage should be cloud-based and scalable

### Accessibility
- Platform must meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards
- Video content must support closed captions
- Mobile-responsive design for all features
